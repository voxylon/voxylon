What is Voxylon?

A community-owned blockchain

Voxylon is an Ethereum-compatible Proof-of-Stake Layer 1 blockchain launching with all the initial supply staked to genesis validators.

Radically fair

Unlike typical crypto launches dominated by VCs and insiders, Voxylon bootstrapped from Ethereum's existing user base. Only accounts that paid at least 0.004 ETH in gas fees by December 31, 2025 could become genesis validators.

Open validator set

Anyone who met the eligibility criteria could register. The registration list is fully transparent and auditable by anyone.

EVM-compatible

Voxylon is fully compatible with Ethereum tooling, smart contracts, and infrastructure. Developers can deploy existing Ethereum applications without modification.
