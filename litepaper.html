Voxylon Litepaper

Overview

Voxylon (VXN) is a community-owned, Ethereum-compatible Proof-of-Stake blockchain, launching with zero premine, no insiders, and no unfair advantages. Voxylon aims to be the fairest Layer 1 blockchain launch in crypto history, leveraging Ethereum's existing user base to bootstrap an entirely decentralized validator set at genesis.

Vision

In a landscape dominated by premined tokens, VC-controlled chains, and centralized validator sets, Voxylon offers a radically fair alternative:

  • No VCs or private sales
  • No team or founder allocation
  • No premined supply
  • 100% of the genesis validators are regular Ethereum users who've paid gas fees
  • The organizers retain zero control over the chain post-launch

Consensus & Architecture

Voxylon is built using Cosmos SDK and ABCIPos, inheriting the battle-tested security and modular architecture of the Cosmos ecosystem. This allows for:

  • Fast finality (typically 2-3 seconds)
  • Horizontal scalability through app-specific chains
  • Interoperability via IBC (Inter-Blockchain Communication)
  • Proposer-based distribution of sequencing work

Ethereum Compatibility

Through Ethereum Virtual Machine (EVM) compatibility via Evmos/Evmos-compatible execution client, Voxylon supports:

  • Solidity smart contracts
  • MetaMask and other Ethereum wallets
  • Ethereum RPC APIs
  • Hardhat, Foundry, and other development tools
  • Existing Ethereum dApps with minimal changes

Genesis Validator Selection

The key innovation of Voxylon is its fair genesis allocation. To become a genesis validator:

  • Your Ethereum account must have paid at least 0.004 ETH in gas fees by December 31, 2025
  • You must generate a BLS12-381 validator key
  • You must sign the validator key with your Ethereum account
  • You must submit your registration before the deadline

This criterion ensures that only real, active Ethereum users can participate, preventing sybil attacks while maintaining true decentralization.

Tokenomics

VXN is the native token of the Voxylon network with the following utilities:

  • Staking: Validators stake VXN to secure the network
  • Transaction fees: Used to pay for gas on the network
  • Governance: Voting rights for protocol upgrades
  • Slashing: Penalties for validator misbehavior

At genesis, exactly 100 VXN will be allocated to each genesis validator's staking pool. There is no presale, no team allocation, and no foundation treasury. The entire supply is distributed to validators based on their participation in the genesis ceremony.

Validator Requirements

Hardware requirements are similar to Ethereum validators:

  • CPU: 4 cores or more
  • RAM: 8 GB (16 GB recommended)
  • Storage: 500 GB SSD (1 TB recommended)
  • Network: 100 Mbps up/down with low latency

Validators are expected to maintain high uptime to maximize rewards and avoid slashing penalties.

Slashing Conditions

To maintain network security, validators face slashing penalties for:

  • Downtime: Extended periods offline
  • Double signing: Signing two different blocks at the same height
  • Equivocation: Sending conflicting messages

Slashing percentages start at 0.01% for minor offenses and can increase for repeated violations.

Roadmap

  • Q4 2025: Validator registration opens
  • Q1 2026: Registration closes, genesis ceremony begins
  • Q2 2026: Mainnet launch
  • Q3 2026: Governance module activation
  • Q4 2026: IBC integration with Cosmos ecosystem

Community & Governance

After genesis, Voxylon is entirely community-owned. Decisions are made through on-chain governance where any validator can propose and vote on changes. The organizers have no special privileges and participate only as regular validators.

Security & Audits

Before mainnet launch, Voxylon will undergo:

  • Third-party security audits
  • Bug bounty program launch
  • Public testnet with incentivized participation
  • Formal verification of critical components
